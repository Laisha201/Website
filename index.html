What to do when visiting St.George Utah

Website By Laisha Ruiz

Hiking

ST.george is known for having great outdoor weather year round and is home to many amazing hikes.

Tuacahn Ampitheatre

Have you ever gone to an outdoor ampitheatre? Visit the Tuacahn ampitheatre where shows like The Little Mermaide, The Wizard Of OZ, and Newsies The Musical will be playing.

Golfing

St.Georges scenic backdrops and pleasent year round weather make it one of the prime destination for golfing in the United States.

State Parks

Snow Canyon, Sand Hollow, and pioneer park are just a few of St.Georges amazing parks. And don't forget about Zion National Park less than an hour away.
